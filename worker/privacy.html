Privacy Policy

Localyse — Figma Plugin · Last updated: February 2026

What Localyse Does

Localyse is a Figma plugin that translates text layers in your design frames into multiple locales. It helps designers create localised versions of their designs directly on the Figma canvas.

Data We Process

When you click Generate Translations, the following data is sent to our translation proxy:

  • Text content from the text layers in your selected frame
  • Layer names (used as context hints for more accurate translations)
  • Target locale code (e.g. fr, ja, ar)
  • Currency codes (if selected, e.g. USD, EUR — used for currency localisation)
  • Your Figma user ID (used solely for rate limiting — 50 requests/day)
  • Your IP address (used as a secondary rate-limiting measure; stored temporarily for up to 24 hours)
We do not collect or log any of your design content. Text is forwarded to the translation services in real time and the response is returned to your plugin. Translated results may be cached on our servers for up to 1 hour to speed up repeated requests — cached data auto-deletes after that. Your Figma user ID and IP address are stored temporarily (up to 24 hours) in counters for rate limiting purposes only.

Third-Party Services

Translations are powered by Microsoft Azure Translator. Your text content is sent to Azure's Translator API to generate translations. Microsoft's use of this data is governed by their Translator transparency note and Privacy Statement. Azure Translator does not store your submitted text after translation is complete.

Translations may also be refined through OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini model for contextual accuracy — improving currency formatting, date localisation, and natural phrasing. When this refinement is enabled, your text content and target locale are sent to the OpenAI API. OpenAI's use of this data is governed by their Privacy Policy. Per OpenAI's API data usage policy, data sent via the API is not used to train their models.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your Figma account email or name
  • Your design files or images
  • Analytics, cookies, or tracking data
  • Any personally identifiable information beyond the anonymous Figma user ID

Rate Limiting

To ensure fair usage, each user is limited to 50 translation requests per day. Your anonymous Figma user ID is stored in a temporary counter that automatically expires after 24 hours. As a secondary abuse-prevention measure, your IP address is also used for rate limiting with a separate daily cap. No other data is associated with these counters.

Data Storage

Localyse does not have a traditional database. The translation proxy is a Cloudflare Worker that uses temporary key-value storage for two purposes:

  • Rate-limit counters (user ID → request count, IP → request count) — auto-delete after 24 hours
  • Translation cache (hashed request → translated result) — auto-deletes after 1 hour

No other data is stored. All temporary entries are automatically purged after their expiry.

Your Rights

Since we do not store personal data beyond a temporary anonymous counter, there is nothing to request deletion of. The counter expires automatically. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us.
